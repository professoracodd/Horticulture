Step 01

Container capacity test

Find out how much water each pot actually needs between irrigations

What to do: Hand-water 6–8 representative pots until water drains freely from the bottom. Wait 30–60 minutes for all drainage to stop — the pots are now at container capacity. Weigh each pot and record the weight. Come back at your normal re-water time and weigh each pot again. Enter both weights below.
